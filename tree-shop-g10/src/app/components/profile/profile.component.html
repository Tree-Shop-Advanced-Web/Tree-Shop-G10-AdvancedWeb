Welcom To The TreeShop

We are a local and online plant nursery based in Surrey Hills offering the Melbourne community access to premium plants, trees and shrubs. Our plants are ideal for both residential, commercial and public-space settings and are available in a range of types, shapes, sizes and colours so that you can design your garden or outdoor space in accordance with your preferences and specifications.

Our broad range of flora include evergreen trees, feature trees suitable for pots, green shade trees, bamboo plants, climbers, shrubs, weeping trees, fruit trees, natives, indoor plants and more. You will be able to convert your indoor or outdoor area into a fantastic green space or urban oasis. Complement your home or business with a colourful, natural and vibrant selection of plants, trees and shrubs that will make your garden feature stand out.
